Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I register an account?

Answer: You can create a new account by clicking the "Register" button at the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required personal information and submit to complete the registration.

Question 2: How do I reset my password?

Answer: On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link, enter your registered email address, and the system will send you an email to reset your password.

Question 3: How do I contact customer service?

Answer: You can send your questions or feedback through the form on the "Contact Us" page, and our customer service team will respond as soon as possible.
